FAQ

What clients ask us most

What is an AI-augmented product design agency?

An AI-augmented product design agency uses AI inside the design workflow — for research synthesis, exploration, prototyping, and design systems — to ship faster and at lower cost. AI accelerates the process; experienced designers stay responsible for product judgment, quality, and every final decision. In short: design with AI, not designing AI products.

How does AI improve the design process?

It removes the slow parts. AI digests research and feedback, generates more directions to explore, builds clickable prototypes quickly, and helps document design systems. That frees experienced designers to spend their time on product thinking, judgment, and craft — so you get more output without dropping quality.

Does AI replace your designers?

No. AI supports the workflow; it never makes the call. Our designers own the problem, product and UX judgment, visual quality, and every final decision. AI explores; designers decide. The tool creates leverage — the team keeps accountability.

What's the difference between an AI-augmented and an AI-powered design agency?

Mostly framing — both mean AI is used in the work. We say "AI-augmented" on purpose: it keeps experienced designers at the center and AI as the multiplier, not the product. We're not an agency that builds AI products; we're a design team that uses AI to work faster.

Is AI-augmented design lower quality?

The opposite, when senior designers steer it. AI lets us explore more directions and iterate more often, so the final work is more considered, not less. Quality control stays fully human: a senior designer reviews and approves every direction and every screen.

Does it cost less than hiring in-house or a traditional agency?

Usually yes, for the output you get. A small senior team with AI leverage delivers the volume of a larger one, without the overhead of an internal team or the layers of a big agency. You add design capacity without permanently adding headcount.

Can you work with our existing product and design system?

Yes — it's a core use case. We add new features and screens, extend and maintain your design system, and deliver clean, dev-ready handoff, so a live product keeps moving and stays consistent as it grows.

Who is AILUME best suited for?

Scaling B2B teams whose roadmap is moving faster than their design capacity — teams that need senior design at volume but don't want to build a large in-house team or wait on a slow agency retainer.
