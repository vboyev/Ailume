About AILUME

A focused studio built from years of shipping real products.

AILUME is an AI-augmented product design agency created by the team behind Halo Lab. It combines proven product experience with a smaller, more focused operating model built for scaling B2B teams.

Meet the team See services

The Reason

The tools changed. The agency model did not.

AI changed how quickly designers can research, explore, prototype, and build systems.

But most agencies responded in one of two ways: they ignored the shift, or turned AI into another vague service line.

We created AILUME to take the change seriously - not as a replacement for designers, and not as a slogan. As a reason to reconsider how a focused product design team should be structured, how much it should be able to deliver, and where human judgment matters most.

Built on Experience

A new studio. Not a new team.

AILUME comes from the people and product experience behind Halo Lab. Halo Lab remains a broad design and development company working across branding, websites, digital products, and engineering. AILUME has a narrower role: AI-augmented product design for scaling B2B teams.

What carries over

Real delivery history.

Years of digital product experience, established design standards, engineering understanding, complex-industry work, and a proven delivery culture.

What is different

A narrower product-design focus.

A smaller team close to the work, productized ways to engage, an AI-augmented production model, and emphasis on design capacity and velocity.

Our Principles

The standards behind the work.

  1. 01
    Judgment matters more than output

    Producing more options is useful only when the team knows which ones deserve to survive.

  2. 02
    AI should remove effort, not accountability

    The team remains responsible for every recommendation, decision, and deliverable.

  3. 03
    Fewer layers create better work

    The closer clients are to the people solving the problem, the less context gets lost.

  4. 04
    Product design must survive engineering

    A strong concept is not enough. The work needs to remain coherent when it becomes a real product.

  5. 05
    Speed should create more room to think

    The benefit of working faster is having more opportunities to test, challenge, and improve the direction.

The People

Small enough to stay close. Experienced enough to lead.

AILUME is structured around experienced product designers who can move between product thinking, UX, interface design, prototyping, systems, and delivery. The exact team changes with the engagement, but the principle does not: the people introduced at the beginning stay involved in the work.

Our Point of View

We are not neutral about the work.

Clients do not hire AILUME to operate Figma on request.

We are expected to question weak assumptions, identify missing decisions, simplify unnecessary complexity, and recommend the direction we believe will work best.

That means we will sometimes disagree. We will explain why, show the alternatives, and connect the decision to the user, product, and business context.

The Ambition

A better model for scaling product design.

A focused team of experienced designers, supported by the right AI workflows, should be able to give scaling companies more design capacity without recreating the cost and complexity of a large agency.
