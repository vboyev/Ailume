About the project

Calm product design for high-stakes financial decisions.

Trafio needed a mobile banking experience that made balances, risk signals, spending behavior, and next actions feel understandable without flattening the complexity behind them.

We shaped the core flows, interface language, and product system so the team could validate the direction before committing to a larger build.

Challenge

Financial interfaces often overload people when they most need clarity.

The work focused on reducing cognitive load, separating urgent signals from everyday finance, and making every screen feel trustworthy enough for repeated use.

Our process

From product assumptions to a clickable direction.

We mapped priority journeys, explored interface patterns with AI-assisted variations, refined the strongest routes manually, and turned the approved direction into a prototype the team could test with users.

Results

A sharper product story and a reusable UI foundation.

The final direction gave Trafio a calmer mobile experience, reusable components, and validated flows that could move into delivery without another round of visual rethinking.
