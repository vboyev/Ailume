About the project

A learning product direction tested before the build cycle.

We designed core learner and teacher experiences around motivation, progress visibility, and bite-sized learning actions that could be tested with users before full production.

The interface balances friendly education patterns with a product system strong enough to scale across lessons, tests, notes, and coaching flows.

Challenge

Retention is won in small moments, not one big feature.

The team needed to understand which interactions would keep learners returning, where progress should be surfaced, and how AI assistance could feel useful without taking over the learning experience.

Our process

Prototype first, then narrow the product bets.

We shaped learning journeys, generated and evaluated alternate UI directions, prototyped the strongest retention patterns, and prepared a design system foundation for future screens.

Results

Validated flows and a clearer product path.

Clawerly received a testable experience for learner retention, teacher visibility, and AI-supported practice, with UI rules ready to carry into new product areas.
