About the project

A tutoring interface that makes progress visible and practice feel lighter.

We designed ASI around learning loops: profile, practice, statistics, lesson context, and the next recommended action.

The system needed to support a friendly student-facing experience while staying disciplined enough for future product growth.

Challenge

AI tutoring needs trust, rhythm, and clear feedback.

The work focused on giving learners a sense of momentum, making progress data useful at a glance, and ensuring AI-driven recommendations felt like support rather than noise.

Our process

We built the foundation around reusable learning states.

We defined learner journeys, explored mobile-first UI patterns, tested hierarchy for progress and practice cards, and translated the strongest direction into a scalable interface system.

Results

A cleaner AI learning product ready to expand.

ASI received a flexible UX/UI foundation for tutoring, statistics, lessons, and practice flows, with system decisions ready for the next product cycle.
