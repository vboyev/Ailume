The Working Model

Less waiting. Fewer layers. Clearer decisions.

Most design delays do not come from the design itself. They come from unclear ownership, long feedback chains, hidden work, and decisions that arrive too late.

AILUME brings the right people into the conversation early, shares work before it becomes precious, and breaks large approvals into smaller decisions your team can make confidently.

One shared workspace

Current thinking stays visible.

Open questions, feedback, and approved directions stay in one place.

Short decision loops

Work is shared while it is easy to improve.

We do not disappear for weeks and return with a presentation.

Direct access

You speak with the designers doing the work.

No chain of account managers between your team and the product decisions.

Engineering involved early

Constraints are discussed before polish.

Important feasibility questions are handled before handoff.

The Delivery Loop

Every cycle moves from question to decision.

  1. 01
    Start with the decision

    Each cycle begins with a clear question: what are we trying to improve, what needs to be decided, and what evidence will show the direction is working?

  2. 02
    Make the work visible early

    We share rough structures, flows, references, and competing directions before spending time polishing them.

  3. 03
    Narrow with context

    Your team brings product knowledge, customer context, constraints, and priorities. AILUME brings product judgment, UX thinking, visual direction, and an external point of view.

  4. 04
    Prepare it to survive the build

    Approved work is developed beyond the presentation layer: states, interactions, content, components, responsive behavior, and implementation details are addressed before engineering begins.

The Production Layer

AI speeds up the work around the decision.

AI is part of our production workflow. It is not part of the approval chain. We use it where it can reduce repetitive effort, widen exploration, or process large amounts of material quickly.

AI can help us

Move faster around research, exploration, and production.

Organize feedback, compare patterns, generate alternative directions, draft interface content, accelerate prototypes, and document components.

Designers remain responsible for

Problem definition, judgment, quality, and recommendations.

The tool creates leverage. The team keeps accountability.

Ready to Build

A finished screen is not a finished design.

  1. 01
    Complete flow

    The primary user flow, important states, edge cases, interactions, and interface content are covered.

  2. 02
    System-ready

    Components and patterns are mapped, responsive behavior is addressed, and implementation details are clear.

  3. 03
    Engineering reviewed

    Engineering has reviewed the proposed solution, and open decisions or assumptions are documented.

Staying Aligned

Enough communication to move. Not enough to slow down.

The exact rhythm depends on the engagement, but the principles stay consistent: async progress updates, focused working reviews, direct access to designers, one place for feedback and decisions, no unnecessary status meetings, and no oversized presentation decks.
