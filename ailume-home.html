FAQ

What clients ask us most

What is an AI-augmented product design agency?

An AI-augmented product design agency uses AI inside the design workflow for research synthesis, exploration, prototyping, and design systems. Strategy, quality, and decisions stay designer-led.

How does AI improve the design process?

AI removes repetitive drag from product design: summarizing research, organizing decisions, generating first directions, and preparing documentation.

Does AI replace your designers?

No. AI supports the workflow. Designers own product judgment, visual quality, and every final recommendation.

What's the difference between an AI-augmented and an AI-powered design agency?

AI-augmented means designers stay responsible for the work while AI increases speed and range. We do not hand strategy to automation.

Is AI-augmented design lower quality?

No. Used well, AI gives senior designers more room to explore and test options before committing to a direction.

Does it cost less than hiring in-house or a traditional agency?

Usually, yes. You get focused senior design capacity without recruiting time, management overhead, or a large agency structure.

Can you work with our existing product and design system?

Yes. We can audit what exists, extend your system, and keep new work aligned with current product constraints.

Who is AILUME best suited for?

Scaling B2B teams whose roadmap is moving faster than their design capacity.
