Frequently asked questions

What is an AI-augmented product design agency?

How does AI improve the design process?

It speeds up research synthesis, exploration, prototyping, and system work, giving designers more time for product thinking and craft.

Does AI replace your designers?

No. AI supports the workflow. Our designers own the direction, judgment, quality, and final outcome.

Who is AILUME best suited for?

Scaling B2B teams whose roadmap is moving faster than their current design capacity.
